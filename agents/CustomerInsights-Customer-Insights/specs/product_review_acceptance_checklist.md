Product Review Acceptance Checklist

Use this one-page checklist to approve or request edits for each published KB.

Checklist items (mark Approve / Request Edits)

1. Tone & urgency
   - Is the headline non-alarming and aligned with company voice?
   - Is the overall tone helpful and not over-technical for customers?

2. Technical accuracy
   - Do mitigation steps correctly reflect supported client behavior?
   - Are API paths, parameters, and example commands accurate?
   - Are references to internal tickets (INT-PR-429, INT-PAG-001) correct?

3. Customer actionability
   - Are the workaround steps actionable for customers without engineering support?
   - Do we provide clear next steps and contact paths for affected customers?

4. Notification decision
   - Approve or deny proactive customer notification.
   - If approving, specify target audience and any additional personalization.

5. Priority & roadmap alignment
   - Does the KB messaging align with Product's roadmap and planned fixes?

6. Final sign-off
   - Product reviewer name, date, and approval (Approve / Request Edits)

Deliverables

- Updated KBs if edits requested (Support will apply changes and re-publish)
- Notification decision and target list if proactive messages required
